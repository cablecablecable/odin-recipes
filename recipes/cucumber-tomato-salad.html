Cucumber Tomato Salad

Description

This refreshing cucumber tomato salad made with sliced cucumber, juicy tomatoes, and onion tossed in a sweet and tangy vinaigrette is easy to prepare, has tons of flavor, and is perfect for a warm summer day.

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Vinegar
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk water, vinegar, oil, sugar, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl until smooth.
  3. Add cucumbers, tomatoes, and onion and stir to coat.
  4. Cover bowl with plastic wrap; refrigerate for at least 2 hours for best flavor results.
  5. Enjoy!
